README for Renegades

Renegades is a collection of small games that combine to form a larger game with a heavy emphasis on story.
It was developed in Unity by myself, Sean Owens, Lilly Scheibelhut, and Jon Lalo and was built in 7 weeks. 

I was the Lead Gameplay Programmer. I developed the Top Down Shooter, Bullet Hell, Base Attack, and Space
Dogfight levels, developed the AI for the Space Dogfight level, coordinated the voice acting for the game, and completed various other tasks.

Cool Things in this Game: 

-6 different battle games 
-games change depending on how many campaigns you've beaten and which commander you choose
-Save files 
-Hand drawn cut scenes 
-Voice over cutscenes 
-Subtitles for voice overs 
-Animated 3D models 
-In mission voice emotes 
-Firefly references
